Sautéed Spinach

Sautéed spinach is a healthy and tasty dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 5 ounces spinach
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • Lemon wedge, for squeezing
  • ground black pepper>

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant but not at all browned.
  3. Add the spinach and salt.
  4. Cook, tossing, for 1 to 2 minutes, or until the spinach is just wilted.
  5. Remove from the heat and season to taste with lemon juice and freshly ground black pepper.
